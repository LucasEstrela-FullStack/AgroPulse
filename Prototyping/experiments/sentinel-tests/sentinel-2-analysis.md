# Sentinel-2 Agricultural Analysis

## Description

Sentinel-2 provides high-resolution optical imagery for vegetation monitoring and crop analysis.

Its multispectral bands enable advanced agricultural indicators such as NDVI.

---

## AgroPulse Application

Sentinel-2 is used to:

- Monitor crop health;
- Generate NDVI maps;
- Identify low-productivity zones;
- Detect vegetation anomalies;
- Support yield forecasting.

---

## Agricultural Indicators

### NDVI

Measures vegetation vigor and health.

### Land Cover Classification

Identifies crop distribution and land use patterns.

### Vegetation Stress Detection

Detects early productivity losses.

---

## Expected Benefits

- Increased productivity;
