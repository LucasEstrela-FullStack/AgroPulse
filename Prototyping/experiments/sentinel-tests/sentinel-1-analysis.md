# Sentinel-1 Agricultural Analysis

## Description

Sentinel-1 provides Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery capable of monitoring agricultural areas regardless of cloud coverage or lighting conditions.

This makes Sentinel-1 extremely valuable for continuous agricultural monitoring.

---

## AgroPulse Application

The platform uses Sentinel-1 data to:

- Monitor soil moisture;
- Detect irrigation efficiency;
- Identify water stress zones;
- Monitor field conditions during cloudy periods;
- Support irrigation prioritization.

---

## Expected Insights

### High Soil Moisture

Indicates healthy irrigation conditions and lower water stress.

### Medium Soil Moisture

Requires monitoring and optimization.

### Low Soil Moisture

Triggers irrigation priority alerts.

---

## Potential Benefits

- Better water management;
- Reduced irrigation costs;
- Early drought detection;
- Increased operational efficiency.
