# Sentinel-3 Agricultural Analysis

## Description

Sentinel-3 provides Land Surface Temperature (LST) data and environmental monitoring capabilities.

Temperature is one of the main indicators affecting crop performance.

---

## AgroPulse Application

The platform uses Sentinel-3 to:

- Monitor heat stress;
- Detect heatwave impacts;
- Support irrigation recommendations;
- Analyze climate anomalies;
- Generate risk alerts.

---

## Key Indicators

### Land Surface Temperature

Measures thermal conditions across agricultural fields.

### Heat Stress Index

Identifies zones under excessive thermal pressure.

### Climate Risk Mapping

Highlights areas vulnerable to productivity losses.

---

## Expected Benefits

- Climate resilience;
- Improved irrigation planning;
- Reduced productivity losses;
- Better risk management.
