# Sentinel Data Validation Experiments

## Overview

This directory contains the technical validation studies conducted during the AgroPulse prototyping phase.

The objective is to evaluate how Copernicus Sentinel satellite data can be transformed into actionable agricultural intelligence for large-scale producers.

The experiments focus on:

- Soil moisture monitoring;
- Crop health analysis;
- Land surface temperature monitoring;
- Climate risk detection;
- Predictive agricultural analytics.

---

## Satellites Evaluated

### Sentinel-1
Radar-based monitoring.

### Sentinel-2
Vegetation and land cover analysis.

### Sentinel-3
Land Surface Temperature (LST) monitoring.

---

## Objective

Validate the feasibility of combining Earth Observation data with Artificial Intelligence to support agricultural decision-making.
