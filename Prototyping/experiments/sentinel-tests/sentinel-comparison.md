# Sentinel Data Comparison

| Satellite | Main Purpose | AgroPulse Use |
|------------|-------------|---------------|
| Sentinel-1 | Soil Moisture & Radar | Irrigation Prioritization |
| Sentinel-2 | Vegetation Monitoring | NDVI & Productivity |
| Sentinel-3 | Surface Temperature | Climate Risk Analysis |

---

## Combined Intelligence

AgroPulse combines all three Sentinel missions to generate more accurate agricultural insights.

### Example

If:

- Soil Moisture ↓
- Surface Temperature ↑
- NDVI ↓

The system identifies a potential productivity risk and recommends immediate action.

---

## Value Generated

The integration of multiple Earth Observation datasets increases prediction accuracy and operational decision quality.
